# Research And Uncertainty

Research only when outside facts matter to the plan.

## Research triggers

- Current product or API capabilities
- Version-sensitive behavior
- Standards, regulations, policies, or official procedures
- Best-practice guidance where source quality matters
- Claims that need citations or source-backed confidence

## Source priorities

- Official documentation
- Maintainer or vendor documentation
- Standards or institution pages
- Primary papers or project repositories

## Uncertainty handling

- Distinguish confirmed facts from synthesis and assumptions.
- If credible sources still conflict or remain incomplete, say that clearly.
- Ask the user for missing context when the unresolved point blocks a reliable plan.
- Do not smooth over uncertainty with confident language.
